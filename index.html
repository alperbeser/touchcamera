Privacy Policy for TouchCamera

At TouchCamera app, accessible from noronaapp.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document clarifies what data is used by TouchCamera app and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact me (alper.beser<at>tu-dortmund.de).

This Privacy Policy applies to our apps and is valid for users of the apps with regards to the information that they shared and/or collect in TouchCamera app. This policy is not applicable to any channels other than TouchCamera.

Consent

By using the TouchCamera app, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Information we collect

TouchCamera collects no face data or any other type of data. TouchCamera asks you to grant permission to your phone's or tablet's camera. In order to recognize hands, the camera data is analyzed on the device and works completely offline. This data is neither sent to us nor to any third party.

The photos you take with the selfie button are saved locally on your device. These photos are neither sent to us nor to any third party. You can forward the created selfie via the iOS share menu to other apps such as WhatsApp.

TouchCamera saves some data locally on your device. These include app settings (e.g. if sound or vibration is turned on) and the number of how often it has reminded you. This data is neither sent to us nor to any third party.

How we use your information

As the app collects no information from you, we cannot use it nor it is forwarded to any third party.
